Ao3 Category Sorter

Parsed and sorted (by total works) across all fandoms for each category and store the results in a CSV and HTML file per category. This makes it easy to see the top fandoms.

  • All (.csv)
  • Anime/Manga (.csv)
  • Books/Literature (.csv)
  • Cartoons/Comics/Graphic Novels (.csv)
  • Movies (.csv)
  • Music / Bands (.csv)
  • Other Media (.csv)
  • Theater (.csv)
  • TV Shows (.csv)
  • Video Games (.csv)
